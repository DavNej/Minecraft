Rules of Minecraft

Minecraft enable you to build constructions out of textured cubes.

You will have 4 types of tools:

Axe, for cutting trees
Secator, for cutting bush and leaves
Pickaxe, for destroying stone,
Shovel for digging dirt and floor.
Clicking on a tool followed by clicking on a tile in the world will remove the tile ans add it to the inventory
You can click on the tile inventory and place it back in the world
